How it works:
  • US Numbers: Enter 10 digits like (555) 123-4567 - country code defaults to +1
  • International Numbers: Check "International number" to enter a custom country code
  • Country detection: International numbers show country word first, US numbers show 3 words
  • Consistent mapping: All words use the same dictionary and mapping system
  • Memorable: Words are easier to remember than numbers
  • Pronounceable: No homophones or confusing sounds
  • Unique: Each phone number maps to distinct words
  • Clean: No offensive terms, plurals, or difficult spellings
  • Shareable: Easier to communicate verbally or in writing
